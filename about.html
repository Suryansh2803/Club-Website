About Abhyudaya

Learn about our mission, mentors, and the journey we are building together.

Who We Are

Abhyudaya is the official AIML Club of G.L. Bajaj, a community where students from all backgrounds collaborate to learn, build, and innovate with Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

Inclusive Community

Open to beginners and experts alike—everyone grows here.

AI/ML Focus

From fundamental concepts to building real-world systems.

Project Driven

We learn by doing—projects, showcases, and demos.

Mentor Guided

Faculty and industry mentors guide our roadmap.

What We Do

Workshops & Bootcamps

Hands-on learning: Python, ML, DL, CV, NLP, MLOps.

Projects & Research

Paper reading, repos, contributions, and campus deployments.

Hackathons

Collaborative building to solve real problems at speed.

Talks & Mentorship

Guest sessions and office hours with mentors and alumni.
